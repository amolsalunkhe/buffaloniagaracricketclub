ABOUT
BNCC

The Buffalo Niagara Cricket Club is the largest cricketing fraternity in the Buffalo-Niagara Region. It was founded in the fall of 2014 by several amateur cricketers in the community who were interested in fostering leather ball cricket in the region.

The club would like to thank all sponsors for their support during the current season. With their generous support we are building facilities and the shaping the future of the club. Our primary aim is to continue to build a dynamic and successful club from Youth Development right through to the 1st XI
